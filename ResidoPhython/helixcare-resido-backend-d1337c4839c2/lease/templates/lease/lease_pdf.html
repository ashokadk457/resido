3.3 Early Termination Policy
Notice Requirement: The Renter may terminate this Rental Agreement before the expiration of the lease term by providing the Landlord with a written notice of at least 30 days prior to the intended move-out date.
Early Termination Fee: In the event of early termination, the Renter agrees to pay an early termination fee equivalent to one month's rent, due on or before the final move-out date.
Conditions for Waiver: The Landlord may, at their discretion, waive the early termination fee in cases of:
• Job relocation (with proof from employer)
• Military deployment (with official orders)
• Health-related issues (with medical documentation)
Security Deposit: The Renter's security deposit will be returned in accordance with the terms outlined in the lease, subject to deductions for unpaid rent, damages, or outstanding charges.
